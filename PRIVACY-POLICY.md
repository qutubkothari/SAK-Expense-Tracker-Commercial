# Privacy Policy for Family Expense Tracker

**Last Updated**: November 17, 2025

SAK Solution ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Family Expense Tracker mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

---

## Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

### Types of Data Collected

#### Personal Data
While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). This includes:

- Phone number (for authentication)
- Name (optional)
- Email address (optional)
- Family member information (names, relationships)

#### Financial Data
To provide expense tracking functionality, we collect:

- Expense amounts and descriptions
- Income amounts and sources
- Payment methods used
- Currency preferences
- Budget information
- Tax categories
- Business expense classifications

#### Voice Data
When using the Voice AI feature:

- Voice recordings are processed to extract expense information
- Recordings are sent to OpenAI's Whisper API for transcription
- Voice data is not permanently stored on our servers
- Transcriptions are processed to create expense entries

#### Usage Data
We may collect information about how the Service is accessed and used, including:

- Device type and operating system
- App usage patterns
- Feature usage statistics
- Error logs and crash reports

## Data Storage and Security

### Where We Store Your Data

Your data is stored securely using:

- **Supabase (PostgreSQL)**: All expense, income, and user data
- **Secure Cloud Infrastructure**: Hosted in secure data centers
- **Encryption**: Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest

### How Long We Keep Your Data

- **Active Accounts**: Data is retained as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Backups**: Backup copies are retained for up to 90 days

### Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

- SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
- Secure authentication using phone number verification
- Regular security audits and updates
- Access controls and monitoring
- Encrypted database storage

**However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.**
